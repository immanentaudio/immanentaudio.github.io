About

FAQ

Common questions

Will these plugins work in my DAW?

Almost certainly. All Immanent Audio plugins come in VST3 and AU formats for both macOS and Windows, which covers Ableton Live, FL Studio, Reaper, Cubase, Studio One, Bitwig, and more. Logic Pro users: grab the AU version.

Can I try before I buy?

Yep. Spectral Agent and Vibrancy both have free demos you can download from their pages, and Harmonic Saturator is 100% free. No email gymnastics required — everything is delivered instantly through Gumroad.

How is Spectral Agent different from a regular dynamic EQ?

A dynamic EQ only reacts to the track it's sitting on. Spectral Agent instances talk to each other across your whole mix — comparing frequency data between tracks, finding collisions, and automatically carving space so your lead elements cut through. No sidechain routing, no manual notch hunting.
