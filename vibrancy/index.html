Vibrancy

Unique Parallel Processing

Vibrancy is a streamlined, reactive parallel processor that effortlessly adds lush reverbs, rhythmic delays, and rich saturation to your tracks while uniquely preserving the clarity of your original audio.

Windows and MacOS-compatible (VST3 and AU)

The Controls

  • Mix: The large central control that seamlessly blends your pristine dry signal with the dynamically processed wet layer.
  • Space: Dial in a lush, optimized reverb tail to push your sound into a massive acoustic environment.
  • Echo: Add feedback delay to create trailing, BPM-synced repeats.
  • Saturate: Push the wet layer through warm, analog-style harmonic waveshaping (from Immanent Audio: Harmonic Saturator) for added grit and presence.
  • Pulse: BPM-synced pumping and rhythmic ducking envelopes for instant movement.
  • Clarify: The secret weapon— dynamic unmasking algorithm (from Immanent Audio: Spectral Agent) that automatically carves frequency space in the wet layer so your dry signal always cuts through.
  • Band: A variable band-pass filter to tightly focus the frequency footprint of the wet layer.
  • Rhythm: Instantly switch the tempo multiplier (from 1/4 up to 4x) to lock the Echo and Pulse effects perfectly to your track's groove.
  • Offset: Apply a slight entrance delay (up to 100ms) to the wet layer, creating a natural chorusing effect that adds immediate width and separation.
  • Gain Adjust: A horizontal trim slider to maintain perfect gain staging on your way out.

In-Depth: Effortless Atmosphere Meets Ultimate Clarity

Vibrancy was designed to be a central home for several effects - allowing you to implement reverb, saturation, delay, and a BPM-synced pulse with a serious emphasis on maintaining clarity and integrity on the original signal. With a simple, easy-to-use UI, Vibrancy will help you dial in the perfect combination of effects on a wet layer to mix into your dry signal with the perfect touch.

As you turn up the Clarify knob, Vibrancy analyzes your dry audio in real-time, finds the most dominant frequencies, and actively carves targeted EQ notches out of the wet layer. This spectral unmasking means your reverb and delay sit perfectly around your audio, rather than on top of it.

Combined with the Band knob—which smoothly sweeps the wet signal into a focused, mid-range bandpass filter—you have unprecedented control over keeping your mix clean. You can push the saturation, crank the reverb, and add rhythmic pumping with the Pulse knob, all without sacrificing the punch and intelligibility of your lead track.

Finally, the Offset control allows you to delay the entire wet signal by just a few milliseconds. This simple addition creates a psychoacoustic chorusing effect, pushing the entire "feel" of the track wider and more full.

Wrapped in a highly reactive interface that responds to your audio with pulsing halos and a dynamic starfield, Vibrancy makes dialing in complex, professional atmospheres faster and more intuitive than ever.
