Overview

[Hook Paragraph for Google and Users]: Traditional saturation plugins apply a flat mathematical curve across your entire signal, causing brittle high-frequencies and muddy low-end build-up. Vibrancy introduces an adaptive, nonlinear saturation algorithm that dynamically shapes harmonics based on the incoming transient velocity and frequency distribution of your audio track.

Whether you need subtle analogue warmth for a vocal bus or aggressive, clipping drive for modern electronic drums, Vibrancy scales seamlessly without introducing unwanted digital aliasing or artifacts.

Key Parameters & Features

  • Adaptive Drive Circuitry: Automatically scales the saturation depth based on internal program volume, protecting micro-transients while thickening dense signals.
  • Odd/Even Harmonic Blend: Complete control over the harmonic generation engine to shape raw technical tape crunch or warm valve tube characteristics.
  • Oversampling Engine: Internal 4x linear-phase oversampling to eliminate digital aliasing reflections in the high-frequency spectrum.

Documentation & Guide

To integrate Vibrancy into your digital audio workstation workflow, place the plugin as an insert effect on your target channel block. Use the Harmonic Bias control to balance third-order harmonics (ideal for punchy bass profiles) or second-order harmonics (optimized for smooth vocal air layers).
